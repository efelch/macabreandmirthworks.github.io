Custom Color Options

Planning a custom order? We recommend selecting colors from Rust-Oleum's line of spray paints, or Varathane's wood stains. We can hand paint using Apple Barrel paints or paint markers, but since this takes longer there is an upcharge.

Don’t see the color you want? I can color-match or source specific paints/stains. Send me a message with your idea, and I’ll help you pick the best finish.
