About

Macabre and Mirthworks creates handcrafted decorations blending Halloween chills with Christmas cheer. Our pieces feature spooky pumpkins, eerie wreaths, gothic ornaments, haunted snow globes, and whimsical holiday-themed curiosities.

Inspired by classic horror films and nostalgic holiday charm, we aim to bring a unique fusion of fright and festivity to your home.
