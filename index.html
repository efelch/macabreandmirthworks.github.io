Services

Custom work and decoration services to bring your ideas to life.

  • Laser Engraving

    Wood wall signs, wreaths, coir doormats, magnets, leather, coasters, cutting boards, glasses and tumblers, pens, corporate and promotional items, cake toppers, ornaments.

  • 3D Printing

    Rapid prototyping and printing. Need favors for a party and want something that's unique? 3D printing is great for organizing kitchens, garages, and more.

  • Custom Apparel

    Customized hats, shirts, tote bags, and more. We use high quality DTF (Direct-to-Film) printing to create lasting designs for your shirts, totes, and other apparel. We can engrave leather patches for hats and bags with whatever design you wish.

    Holiday decorating consultation for your yard or house. Bespoke props and decor made to order. We're that house in our neighborhood, we can help you become that for yours!

About Us

Edward and Monica Felch

Macabre and Mirthworks is a small garage-based duo crafting seasonal decor and home goods in the Lowcountry. We love decorating! Our yard and house transform for the season, and we hope to be part of your family's traditions!

Our work is largely made to order since most of it is custom. Reach out if you want something specific or wish to consult on your home or business' decorations.

FAQ

What are your capabilities?

We have an industrial laser engraver with a bed capacity of 18x36" and a conveyor feeder to allow us to create large items such as door welcome signs. We can 3D print designs and also have a vinyl cutter for creating custom mugs, shirts, and more. Our garage is equipped with a complete woodworking shop, and we have products for creating Halloween and Christmas decorations.

Do you take custom orders?

Yes. Send us a DM on Instagram or a message on Etsy with what you’re looking for and your timeline.

Where can I buy your products?

We sell through our Etsy shop. This site is a portfolio and information hub — inventory and checkout are handled on Etsy. I also attend a limited set of craft fairs.

What’s your processing time?

Ready-to-ship items typically go out within 3–5 business days. Custom orders vary by complexity; we’ll provide an estimate when you contact us.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently, we ship within the United States.

Do you offer consultation?

Yes, we are prepared to come to your home or business within the Tri-County Area and help with making your holiday the best it can by offering plans for power, lighting, decorations, and atmospheric effects!
